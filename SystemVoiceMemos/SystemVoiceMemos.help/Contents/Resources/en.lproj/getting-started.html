Getting Started

Set up SystemVoiceMemos and make your first recording

Requirements

  • macOS 15.0 or later
  • Screen Recording permission (required for system audio capture)
  • Optional: Microphone permission (for recording external audio devices)
  • Optional: Location permission (for city-based recording naming)

First Launch

When you first launch SystemVoiceMemos, you'll see the Welcome Guide. This onboarding flow walks you through granting the necessary permissions.

Screen Recording Permission

This permission is required to capture system audio. SystemVoiceMemos uses ScreenCaptureKit to access the audio stream from your displays — no video is recorded.

  1. Click Grant next to Screen Recording in the Welcome Guide
  2. macOS will open System Settings
  3. Find SystemVoiceMemos in the list and enable the toggle
  4. Return to SystemVoiceMemos — the permission should now show as granted
Note: If you previously denied Screen Recording permission, you may need to click the Settings button to manually open System Settings.

Microphone Permission (Optional)

If you want to record external audio devices (microphones, instruments) alongside system audio, grant Microphone access.

  1. Click Grant next to Microphone Access
  2. When prompted, click Allow

Location Permission (Optional)

SystemVoiceMemos can automatically name your recordings based on your current city. This is entirely optional and can be enabled later in Settings.

Making Your First Recording

  1. Click New Recording in the menu bar or press Cmd+N
  2. The recording indicator appears — SystemVoiceMemos is now capturing system audio
  3. Play audio on your Mac (music, videos, system sounds — anything)
  4. Click Stop Recording when done
  5. Your recording appears in the recordings list with a timestamp-based name
Important: Only one app can capture system audio at a time. If you have other screen recording or audio capture apps running, SystemVoiceMemos may not be able to start recording.

Understanding the Interface

Main Window

  • Sidebar — Browse recordings by folder or view all recordings
  • Recordings List — Shows all recordings with title, date, and duration
  • Playback Controls — Play, pause, skip, and seek through recordings
  • Recording Indicator — Shows when a recording is in progress

Menu Bar

  • File — New Recording, Delete, Show in Finder, Open in QuickTime
  • View — Toggle Sidebar (Cmd+Option+S)
  • Playback — Play/Pause, skip forward/back, play selection
  • Help — Open this help book or show the Welcome Guide

Where Recordings Are Stored

By default, SystemVoiceMemos stores recordings in:

~/Library/Containers/com.arambdev.SystemVoiceMemos/Data/Documents/Recordings/

To reveal this folder in Finder, right-click any recording and choose Show in Finder.
