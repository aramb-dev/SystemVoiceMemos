Quick Start

  1. Grant Screen Recording permission when prompted (required for system audio capture)
  2. Click New Recording or press Cmd+N to start recording
  3. Click Stop Recording when done — your memo is saved automatically
  4. Double-click any recording to play it back in the app

Key Features

  • System Audio Capture — Records any audio playing on your Mac using ScreenCaptureKit
  • Multi-Track Recording — Record system audio and microphone simultaneously
  • Stem Export — Export separate audio tracks for further production
  • In-App Playback — Full playback controls with skip forward/back 15 seconds
  • Folder Organization — Keep your recordings organized with custom folders
  • Location-Based Naming — Automatically name recordings by city
  • Local & Private — All recordings stored locally on your Mac
