Playback

Listen to your recordings with in-app controls and keyboard shortcuts

Playing Recordings

To play a recording, simply double-click it in the recordings list. Alternatively:

  • Select a recording and press Space
  • Select a recording and press Return
  • Click the Play button in the playback controls

The playback controls appear when a recording is selected, showing:

  • Play/Pause button — Toggle playback
  • Time display — Current position and total duration
  • Progress bar — Seek to any position in the recording
  • Skip buttons — Jump forward or back 15 seconds

Playback Controls

Play/Pause

Toggle playback with:

  • Space — Play/pause when a recording is selected
  • The Play/Pause button in the playback controls

Skip Forward/Back

Jump through the recording:

  • Option+← — Skip back 15 seconds
  • Option+→ — Skip forward 15 seconds
  • Click the skip buttons in the playback controls
  • Click directly on the progress bar to seek to that position

Play Selection

If you've selected a portion of the waveform (when available), press Return to play just that section.

Volume

Adjust playback volume using your Mac's system volume controls or the volume slider in the playback controls (if available).

Opening Recordings Externally

Sometimes you may want to open a recording in another application:

Show in Finder

Reveal the recording file in Finder:

  • Right-click the recording and choose Show in Finder
  • Select the recording and use the File menu → Show in Finder

Open in QuickTime Player

Open the recording directly in QuickTime Player:

  • Right-click the recording and choose Open in QuickTime Player
  • Select the recording and use the File menu → Open in QuickTime Player
Note: You can also drag recordings from Finder into any audio application (GarageBand, Logic Pro, Audacity, etc.) for further editing.

Multi-Track Playback

When you have multi-track recordings (system audio + microphone), SystemVoiceMemos plays both tracks mixed together. The relative volume balance depends on the levels during recording.

For separate track control, use stem export and open the files in a DAW or audio editor.

Playback Troubleshooting

No sound during playback

  • Check your Mac's system volume is not muted
  • Verify the recording file actually has audio (open in QuickTime)
  • Check if any external audio devices are selected as output

Playback is choppy or stutters

  • Close other apps that may be using significant CPU or disk I/O
  • Try opening the file in QuickTime to rule out an issue with the recording

Can't select or play a recording

  • Ensure the recording file still exists in the recordings directory
  • Try restarting SystemVoiceMemos if the UI seems unresponsive

Deleting Recordings

To delete a recording you no longer need:

  1. Select the recording in the list
  2. Press Cmd+Delete or choose Delete Recording from the File menu
  3. Confirm the deletion
Warning: Deleted recordings are permanently removed and cannot be recovered. The file is deleted from disk, not just removed from the list.
