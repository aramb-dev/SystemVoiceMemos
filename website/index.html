How It Works

Get started in seconds with these simple steps

1

Download & Install

Download the DMG, drag to Applications, and launch. Grant Screen Recording permission when prompted.

2

Click Record

Hit the record button to start capturing system audio. A timer shows your recording duration.

3

Save & Organize

Stop recording and your audio is automatically saved. Organize into folders and play back anytime.
