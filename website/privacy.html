Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

System Voice Memos is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our application handles your data.

Data Collection

System Voice Memos does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All recordings are stored locally on your Mac and are never uploaded to any server. We do not have access to your recordings or any other data on your device.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Audio recordings you create
  • Folder organization preferences
  • App settings and preferences

This data remains on your device and is under your complete control. You can delete it at any time by removing recordings from the app or uninstalling the application.

Permissions

System Voice Memos requires the following system permissions:

  • Screen Recording: Required to capture system audio on macOS
  • Microphone (optional): Only if you want to record external audio

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and are never used to collect or transmit data.

Third-Party Services

System Voice Memos uses Sparkle for automatic updates. Sparkle may check for updates by contacting our GitHub releases page. No personal information is transmitted during this process.

Open Source

System Voice Memos is open source. You can review the complete source code on GitHub to verify our privacy practices.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
